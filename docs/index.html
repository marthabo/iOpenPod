What It Does

Everything you need to keep your iPod alive — no iTunes, no hassle.

🎵

Any Format In, iPod Out

FLAC, OGG, WMA, MP3, AAC — drop in whatever you have. Non-native formats get transcoded to ALAC or AAC automatically.

📻

Podcasts Built In

Search, subscribe, and download podcast episodes right inside iOpenPod. Sync them to your iPod without a separate app.

🎧

ListenBrainz Scrobbling

Sign into ListenBrainz and your listening history scrobbles automatically every time you sync. Keep your music profile current.

📚

More Than Music

Audiobooks, movies, and TV shows are all supported. Your iPod sorts them into the right categories automatically.

🖱️

Drag and Drop

Don't need full sync? Just drag files into the app and they'll land on your iPod — no setup, no fuss.

📊

Play Counts & Ratings

Listen on your iPod, plug it in, and your play counts, ratings, and skip counts sync back to your PC library.

🖼️

Album Art Included

Art is pulled from your files, resized, and written in the iPod's native format. No extra steps required.

✅

Review Before Syncing

Every sync shows you exactly what's changing — adds, removes, updates — with checkboxes for each item.

🛡️

Backup & Rollback

Your iPod database is snapshotted before every sync. One click to undo if something goes wrong.

📋

Playlists & Smart Playlists

Browse, create, and manage playlists. Smart playlists with rule-based filtering are supported too.

🌗

Light & Dark Mode

Choose your theme. The UI scales beautifully on high-DPI retina displays and low-res monitors alike.

🔄

Auto-Updater

iOpenPod checks for updates automatically and can update itself right from the app. Always stay current.

For Developers

Want to hack on iOpenPod? Clone the repo and get running in seconds.

Requires Python 3.13+ and uv.

git clone https://github.com/TheRealSavi/iOpenPod.git cd iOpenPod uv sync uv run python main.py

That's it. uv sync installs all dependencies into a virtual environment automatically.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sync my iPod without iTunes?

Download iOpenPod from the releases page, extract it, and run it. Connect your iPod via USB, select your media folder, and click sync — no iTunes or Python needed.

Do I need Python to use iOpenPod?

No. The releases page provides precompiled binaries for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Just download, extract, and run. Python is only needed if you want to contribute to development.

Can I put FLAC files on an iPod?

Yes. iOpenPod automatically converts FLAC to Apple Lossless (ALAC) during sync, so your iPod plays them natively with no quality loss.

Does it work on Linux and macOS?

Yes. Precompiled downloads are available for Windows, macOS, and Linux. Just extract and run.

Which iPod models are supported?

All iPod Classic generations, iPod Mini, iPod Photo, and iPod Nano 1G–7G have full read/write support. Shuffle support is coming soon.

Will syncing erase my iPod?

No. iOpenPod shows you exactly what will change before writing anything, and backs up your database before every sync. Roll back with one click.

Can I just drag files onto my iPod without syncing?

No, the iPod needs to have its database updated. But you can drag and drop files into iOpenPod and they'll be added to your iPod. No sync setup required.
